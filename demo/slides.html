Cuecard

iPad-controlled Presentation
Framework for Node.js

The problem

  • Browser-based presentations are awesome.
  • Hunching over your laptop isn't awesome.
  • Remotes force you to turn around to see your slides.

Another problem

Lots of frameworks to choose from:

  • Fathom.js (mine)
  • Reveal.js (this one)
  • Deck.js

Not to mention...

Each requires its own styles and slide boilerplate...

(I just wanted to show off the vertical slides)

The solution

Cuecard!

  • Use a tablet as your remote.
  • Drive any framework with a simple adapter.
  • Presenter view allows for custom styles and markup.
  • Minimises boilerplate, only slide markup required.

Simple server.js

var cuecard = require('cuecard');

cuecard.create({
    port: 3000,
    remoteUrl: '/remote',

    adapter: 'reveal',
    title: 'My Cuecard Presentation',
    slides: __dirname + '/slides.html',
    styles: __dirname + '/slides.css'
});

Cuecard in action

Run 'node server.js'

Go to http://localhost:3000/remote to control

Connect client to http://localhost:3000

Under the hood

  • Runs on Node.js
  • WebSockets via Socket.io for real-time goodness
  • Grunt for linting and testing
